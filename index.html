About Us

Established in April 2025, Hancock & Brown Enterprises Ltd is a UK-based private limited company dedicated to helping modern businesses streamline and optimise their operations through smart automation workflows.

We specialise in designing and deploying low-code and no-code solutions using platforms such as n8n, Zapier, Make (Integromat), and custom API connections. With a strong foundation in operational automation, we enable individuals, teams, and enterprises to reduce manual overhead and focus on growth. Our team combines technical knowledge with strategic insight to deliver scalable, efficient, and highly customisable automation systems.

Our dedication to detail and expert service has earned us a reputation for quality — and we’re proud to help our clients work smarter, not harder.
